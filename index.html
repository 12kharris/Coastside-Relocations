About Us


We are a luxury relocations company. We provide the highest quality of accommodation for holiday goers and the work force. Our area of specialty is coastal properties in the Thanet district of Kent. Thanet is famous for its beaches, the friendly locals and its many lively coastal towns and so every one of our holiday homes is located within a few minutes of the sea!
